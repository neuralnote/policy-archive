Overview

What is the application?
neuralnote.io is an AI-powered personal notebook and interactive knowledgebase builder that can help you explore almost any topic of interest in an organised way, allowing you to ask questions, take notes, form checklists, and more.

What does the application do?
neuralnote.io integrates with OpenAI to provide you with a personal space for you to:

  • Create pages (and bookmark them)
  • Ask questions to receive structured AI responses about a topic or question. When a structured response is added to your page, it will often highlight various concepts within the response as hyperlinks that you can click on to drill down further into a particular concept. Clicking on a hyperlink will create a new nested page that explores the selected concept further (and will often contain its own hyperlinks). This allows you to explore a topic in a top-down fashion, automatically building a knowledgebase for you about a particular topic through a tree of nested pages.
  • You can also ask questions to receive free-form AI responses that post to your current page. These generally won't contain hyperlinks, but they allow you to interact in a more free-form way.
  • You may also add notes and smart checklists to your pages.
More features are on the way!

What are the benefits of using the application?

  • With AI, you can co-create personal knowledgebases allowing you to explore almost any topic
  • You can maintain an online notebook of your thoughts, and plan projects by building out pages with notes, checklists, and AI generated posts
